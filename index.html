Nature

Taking photos of the nature is anot always easy. Though most of the locations are already beautiful, the lighting is one of the challenges. Finding the right light angle and the right color tone is very challenging.

Urban

Taking photos of the city is usually best at night. The light from the city makes the city look very outstanding in the dark sky. The challenge of this type pf photography is the time needed for the camera to take the light in. A lot of night time photography takes more than 30 seconds per one shot. If the camera is not very still, the picture will come out blurly
